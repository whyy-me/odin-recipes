briyani

Description

Biryani is a flavorful, aromatic rice dish made with layers of marinated meat, basmati rice, and spices.

It's a festive favorite across India and beyond.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g mutton, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 4-5 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (cinnamon, cardamom, cloves)
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Saffron strands (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking
  • Water for cooking

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil or ghee and fry the sliced onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnishing.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies to the pot, saut√© for a minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add mutton pieces, yogurt, whole spices, and salt. Cook until the meat is tender.
  6. Layer the soaked rice over the cooked mutton. Add fresh coriander and mint leaves on top.
  7. If using saffron, dissolve it in warm milk and drizzle over the rice.
  8. Add enough water to cover the rice. Cover tightly and cook on low heat for about 30-40 minutes until the rice is cooked through.
  9. Garnish with fried onions before serving.
  10. Serve hot with raita or salad.
